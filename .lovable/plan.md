

# Motherland Properties Website
A minimalist, bilingual real estate website with elegant design and full interactive features.

---

## Design Philosophy
- **Color Palette**: Emerald green primary, gold accents, clean white backgrounds
- **Typography**: Modern, readable fonts optimized for both English and Bengali
- **Style**: Generous whitespace, subtle animations, premium feel
- **Mobile-First**: 80% of visitors will be on mobile - prioritizing that experience

---

## Pages & Features

### 1. Home Page (হোম)
- Hero section with fullscreen video/image slider showcasing the projects
- Animated statistics (plots sold, happy customers, years of trust)
- Quick project overview cards with hover effects
- Customer testimonials carousel
- Trust badges (RAJUK, REHAB certifications)
- Language toggle (EN/বাং) in navigation

### 2. Projects Page (প্রকল্প সমূহ)
- **Nimtola Smart City** - dedicated section with gallery
- **Purbachal Iconic Lake City** - dedicated section with gallery
- Interactive plot availability map (green=available, yellow=booked, red=sold)
- Advanced filters: plot size, road width, corner plots, price range
- Each plot shows key details on hover/click

### 3. EMI Calculator (কিস্তি ক্যালকুলেটর)
- Beautiful calculator interface
- Select plot value, down payment percentage
- Choose installment period: 12, 24, or 36 months
- Real-time calculation display with monthly breakdown
- Share/print quote functionality

### 4. Amenities Page (নাগরিক সুবিধা)
- Visual showcase of civic amenities
- Schools & Colleges nearby
- Mosques and community spaces
- Parks and lake views
- Healthcare facilities
- Each with location on embedded Google Map

### 5. Trust Center (আইনি নথিপত্র)
- RAJUK approval documents display
- Environmental clearance certificates
- REHAB membership proof
- Land records information (CS, RS, BS)
- Clear timeline: Booking → Down Payment → Registration → Handover

### 6. About Us (আমাদের সম্পর্কে)
- Company vision and mission
- Leadership team
- Why choose Motherland Properties
- Company history timeline

### 7. Contact (যোগাযোগ)
- Floating WhatsApp chat button (always visible)
- Contact form with inquiry type selection
- Office locations with Google Maps
- NRB (Non-Resident Bangladeshi) dedicated contact section
- Social media links
